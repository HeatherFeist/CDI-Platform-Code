**OPERATING AGREEMENT**
**OF**
**[BUSINESS NAME], LLC**

This Operating Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into effective as of [Date], by and between [Business Name], LLC, a [State] limited liability company (the "Company"), and [Business Incubation LLC] (the "Initial Member").

### ARTICLE I: ORGANIZATION

**1.1 Formation.**
The Company has been organized as a [State] limited liability company by filing Articles of Organization with the Secretary of State of [State].

**1.2 Name.**
The name of the Company is **[Business Name], LLC**.

**1.3 Purpose.**
The purpose of the Company is to engage in any lawful act or activity for which limited liability companies may be formed under the Act, specifically including but not limited to [Specific Industry/Business Activity], and to prepare the Company for sale via auction on the CDI Marketplace.

**1.4 Principal Office.**
The principal office of the Company is located at [Address].

**1.5 Registered Agent.**
The registered agent of the Company is [Agent Name], located at [Address].

### ARTICLE II: MEMBERSHIP AND CAPITAL

**2.1 Initial Member.**
The sole member of the Company upon formation is **[Business Incubation LLC]**, a wholly-owned subsidiary of [Nonprofit Name].

**2.2 Capital Contributions.**
The Initial Member has made a capital contribution of **$[Amount]** in cash to the Company.

**2.3 Seed Capital Account.**
The Company shall maintain a dedicated "Seed Capital" bank account containing the Initial Capital Contribution. This capital is intended to be transferred to the eventual purchaser of the Company to fund initial operations.

**2.4 Merchant Coin Wallet.**
The Company shall establish and maintain a digital wallet for the holding of Merchant Coins. The Initial Member shall allocate **[Amount]** Merchant Coins to this wallet prior to any transfer of ownership.

### ARTICLE III: MANAGEMENT

**3.1 Management by Member.**
Until the Transfer Date (as defined below), the Company shall be managed by the Initial Member. The Initial Member may appoint officers (e.g., President, Treasurer) to handle day-to-day operations.

**3.2 Business Incubation Committee.**
The Initial Member designates the "Business Incubation Committee" of [Nonprofit Name] to oversee the development, strategy, and preparation of the Company for auction.

### ARTICLE IV: TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP (THE "AUCTION")

**4.1 Intent to Transfer.**
It is the express intent of the Initial Member to transfer 100% of the Membership Interest in the Company to a qualified third party (the "Buyer") through a competitive auction process on the CDI Marketplace.

**4.2 Transfer Date.**
The "Transfer Date" shall be the date on which the closing of the sale to the Buyer occurs.

**4.3 Admission of New Member.**
Upon the closing of the sale, the Buyer shall be admitted as the sole Member of the Company, and the Initial Member shall withdraw as a Member. The Buyer shall execute a Joinder to this Agreement or an Amended and Restated Operating Agreement.

**4.4 Conditions of Transfer.**
Any transfer of Membership Interest is conditioned upon the Buyer's agreement to:
(a) Maintain the Company's participation in the CDI Merchant Coin ecosystem for a minimum of 5 years;
(b) Accept the Company's Merchant Coin as a form of payment; and
(c) Participate in the CDI Support Program.

### ARTICLE V: DISTRIBUTIONS AND TAX MATTERS

**5.1 Distributions.**
Prior to the Transfer Date, no distributions shall be made to the Initial Member, as all capital is intended to remain in the Company for the benefit of the Buyer.

**5.2 Tax Treatment.**
The Company shall be treated as a disregarded entity for federal income tax purposes while it has a single Member.

### ARTICLE VI: DISSOLUTION

**6.1 Events of Dissolution.**
The Company shall dissolve upon:
(a) The written consent of the Member;
(b) The entry of a decree of judicial dissolution; or
(c) The failure to successfully auction or transfer the Company within [Number] months of formation, at the discretion of the Initial Member.

**6.2 Distribution of Assets.**
Upon dissolution, assets shall be distributed in the following order:
(a) To creditors, including Members who are creditors;
(b) To the Member.

### ARTICLE VII: MISCELLANEOUS

**7.1 Governing Law.**
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [State].

**7.2 Amendments.**
This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by the Member.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the undersigned has executed this Operating Agreement as of the date first written above.

[Business Incubation LLC]
