## ‚ùì **FAQ for Retail Partners**

### **Q: What types of items can we list?**
**A:** Anything construction/home improvement related:
- Scratch & dent appliances (working)
- Damaged packaging (product intact)
- Clearance/discontinued items
- Customer returns (tested/working)
- End-of-season overstock
- Display models
- Bulk pallets of mixed items

### **Q: Who are the buyers?**
**A:** Licensed contractors, verified through our nonprofit:
- General contractors
- Specialty trades (electricians, plumbers, etc.)
- Remodeling companies
- Property managers
- NOT retail consumers (B2B only)

### **Q: What if an item doesn't sell?**
**A:** You can:
- Lower the starting bid
- Relist automatically
- Donate to nonprofit (tax deduction)
- We'll pick up for proper recycling if needed

### **Q: What about liability?**
**A:** 
- Items sold "as-is" (clearly disclosed)
- No warranties (standard for clearance)
- Contractors sign waiver at pickup
- Your existing liability insurance covers loading dock transactions
- We can add you as additional insured on our policy

### **Q: How do we handle pickup?**
**A:**
- Contractors schedule pickup appointments
- They receive authorization code from our system
- Show code at your receiving department
- Your team verifies code, releases item
- Takes 2-3 minutes per transaction
- Can batch pickups during slow times

### **Q: Do we need special software?**
**A:** No.
- Option 1: Email us photos/info (we handle rest)
- Option 2: Use our simple web portal (2 min per item)
- Option 3: API integration (IT team, one-time setup)

### **Q: What if we want to stop?**
**A:** No long-term contract.
- 30-day cancellation notice
- No fees or penalties
- Partnership can pause/resume anytime
