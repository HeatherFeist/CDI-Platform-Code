- **Endgame:** Wealthy but alone, competing forever

- You work harder for others than traditional selfishness
- You profit less immediately than traditional selfishness
- **YET you end up happier, wealthier, and more fulfilled**

**Why?**
- When everyone around you succeeds, opportunities multiply
- When your community thrives, you're embedded in prosperity
- When helpers become contractors, they remember who taught them
- When you give without expectation, you receive without asking
