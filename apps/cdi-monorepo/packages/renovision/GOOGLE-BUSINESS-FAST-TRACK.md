## **Edge Cases & Solutions**

### **Q: What if someone fake-claims a business?**
**A:** Google OAuth verification prevents this. You must prove you manage the business through Google My Business.

### **Q: What if business has 51+ reviews but they're fake/bought?**
**A:** Google's anti-fraud systems already filter fake reviews. If Google counts them, we count them. Plus, actual network job performance will reveal the truth quickly.

### **Q: What if contractor's Google Business is inactive/outdated?**
**A:** Last sync timestamp shows staleness. Prompt: "Sync your Google Business (last updated 6 months ago)" with refresh button.

### **Q: What if they have multiple business locations?**
**A:** Let them choose which location to connect, or sum reviews across all locations under same owner.

### **Q: What if they don't have Google Business?**
**A:** Offer to help them create one! "Don't have a Google Business Profile? We'll help you set one up - it's free and boosts your visibility." (Helps THEM + helps US get more Fast-Track signups later)

### **Q: What if review count drops (deleted reviews)?**
**A:** Tier stays same (never downgrade). But show warning: "Your Google reviews decreased from 87 to 82. Keep quality high!"

-- How many contractors used Fast-Track?
SELECT 
  COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE google_business_verified = true) as fast_track_signups,
  COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE google_business_verified = false) as traditional_signups,
  ROUND(
    COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE google_business_verified = true)::NUMERIC / 
    COUNT(*)::NUMERIC * 100, 
    1
  ) as fast_track_percentage
FROM profiles
WHERE role = 'contractor'
AND created_at >= '2025-11-01';

-- Average time to Platinum (Fast-Track vs Traditional)
SELECT 
  CASE 
    WHEN google_business_verified THEN 'Fast-Track'
    ELSE 'Traditional'
  END as path,
  AVG(
    EXTRACT(DAY FROM (
      -- When did they hit Platinum?
      COALESCE(google_business_import_date, created_at + INTERVAL '180 days')
    ) - created_at)
  ) as avg_days_to_platinum
FROM profiles
WHERE role = 'contractor'
GROUP BY path;

-- Fast-Track: 0.003 days (5 minutes)
-- Traditional: 180+ days (6 months)
