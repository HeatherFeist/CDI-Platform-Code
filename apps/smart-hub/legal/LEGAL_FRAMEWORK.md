## üè¢ Organizational Structure

### Nonprofit Entity

**Recommended Structure**: 501(c)(3) or 501(c)(6)

**501(c)(3) - Charitable Organization**
- **Pros**: Tax-exempt, tax-deductible donations, grants eligible
- **Cons**: Limited political activity, strict charitable purpose
- **Best For**: Community development, education, economic empowerment focus

**501(c)(6) - Business League**
- **Pros**: Member services, industry advancement, networking
- **Cons**: Donations not tax-deductible, more regulatory scrutiny
- **Best For**: Business incubation, merchant support, marketplace operation

**Recommended**: **501(c)(3)** with mission focused on:
- Economic empowerment through entrepreneurship
- Small business development and support
- Community wealth building
- Financial literacy and education

### Subsidiary Structures

**Business Incubation LLC** (Wholly-owned by nonprofit)
- Creates and holds businesses until auction
- Shields nonprofit from business liabilities
- Allows for business operations separate from charitable activities
- Profits flow back to nonprofit

**Marketplace Operations LLC** (Wholly-owned by nonprofit)
- Operates CDI Marketplace platform
- Handles merchant coin transactions
- Manages auction system
- Generates revenue for nonprofit

## üìÑ Auction Documents

### 1. Auction Terms & Conditions

**Key Provisions**:

```markdown
AUCTION TERMS & CONDITIONS

1. ELIGIBILITY
   - Must be CDI Marketplace member (Builder or Innovator tier)
   - Must complete "Business Ownership 101" training
   - Must verify financial capability
   - Must agree to merchant coin integration

2. BIDDING RULES
   - Minimum bid: [Amount specified in listing]
   - Bid increments: $1,000 minimum
   - Auto-extend: +10 minutes if bid in final 5 minutes
   - Maximum 3 auto-extends
   - Bids are binding and irrevocable

3. WINNER OBLIGATIONS
   - 10% deposit due within 3 business days
   - Due diligence period: 14 days
   - Final payment due before transfer
   - All payments non-refundable after due diligence

4. WHAT'S INCLUDED
   - Complete legal entity with EIN
   - Business bank account with seed capital
   - Merchant coin wallet with initial allocation
   - All equipment, inventory, and assets listed
   - Vendor contracts and relationships
   - Domain names and digital assets
   - 5-year support package (Foundation tier)

5. AS-IS SALE
   - Business sold "as-is" with no warranties
   - Financial projections are estimates only
   - Buyer responsible for own due diligence
   - No refunds after transfer completion

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
   - Mediation required before litigation
   - Arbitration clause (if disputes arise)
   - Governing law: [State]
   - Venue: [County, State]

7. PAYMENT TERMS
   - Deposit: 10% of winning bid (3 business days)
   - Final payment: 90% of winning bid (after due diligence)
   - Accepted methods: ACH, wire transfer, merchant coins (up to 50%)
   - Escrow required for transactions over $50,000

8. TRANSFER TIMELINE
   - Due diligence: 14 days after deposit
   - Final payment: 7 days after due diligence approval
   - Transfer completion: 7 days after final payment
   - Total timeline: Approximately 28 days
```

## üîí Risk Mitigation & Liability Protection

### For the Nonprofit

**1. Separate Legal Entities**
- Business Incubation LLC shields nonprofit
- Each created business is separate entity
- Liabilities don't flow to nonprofit

**2. Proper Disclosures**
- "As-is" sale provisions
- No guarantees of success
- Financial projections are estimates
- Buyer assumes all risks post-transfer

**3. Insurance Coverage**
- General liability insurance
- Directors & officers insurance
- Professional liability insurance
- Cyber liability insurance

**4. Compliance**
- Regular legal audits
- State registration maintenance
- Tax compliance (990 filings)
- Charitable solicitation registration (if applicable)

### For Buyers

**1. Due Diligence Period**
- 14 days to review everything
- Right to terminate with refund
- Access to all business records
- Professional advisor consultation encouraged

**2. Representations & Warranties**
- Seller makes specific promises
- Indemnification if breached
- Escrow protection

**3. Support Services**
- 5-year guidance reduces risk
- Mentorship and resources
- Community support

**4. Exit Options**
- Buyback program (if business fails in Year 1)
- Resale on marketplace
- Dissolution procedures

## üìä Tax Considerations

### For the Nonprofit

**Unrelated Business Income Tax (UBIT)**
- Auction proceeds may be UBIT if "regularly carried on"
- Consult tax advisor on frequency limits
- Possible exemption if furthers exempt purpose

**Charitable Purpose Documentation**
- Document how program furthers mission
- Economic empowerment
- Job creation
- Community development

**990 Reporting**
- Report auction revenue
- Report support services revenue
- Disclose related party transactions

### For Buyers

**Business Acquisition**
- Asset purchase vs. entity purchase
- Depreciation and amortization
- Goodwill allocation
- State tax implications

**Ongoing Operations**
- Business entity tax treatment
- Estimated tax payments
- Sales tax collection
- Payroll taxes

**Merchant Coin Transactions**
- Coins treated as property (IRS guidance)
- Capital gains/losses on coin value changes
- Reporting requirements

## üö® Red Flags & Warnings

### Avoid These Pitfalls

**1. Securities Violations**
- Merchant coins could be deemed securities
- Howey Test: Investment of money, common enterprise, expectation of profit from others' efforts
- **Mitigation**: Coins are utility tokens (discounts), not investments
- Consult securities attorney

**2. Money Transmitter Issues**
- Facilitating coin exchanges may require licenses
- State-by-state requirements
- **Mitigation**: Use licensed payment processor
- Consult financial services attorney

**3. Franchise Law Violations**
- Support + trademark + fees = possible franchise
- FTC Franchise Rule compliance required
- **Mitigation**: No trademark license, support is optional upgrade
- Consult franchise attorney

**4. Tax-Exempt Status Jeopardy**
- Too much commercial activity
- Private benefit to individuals
- **Mitigation**: Document charitable purpose, limit auction frequency
- Consult nonprofit tax attorney
