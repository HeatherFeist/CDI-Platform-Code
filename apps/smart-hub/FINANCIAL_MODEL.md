## ðŸ’° Revenue Streams

### 1. Auction Proceeds (Primary Revenue)

**The "Crowd-Funded" Advantage**:
- **Seed Capital**: Donated by community (Not an expense for nonprofit).
- **Auction Revenue**: 100% of the winning bid goes to the nonprofit.
- **Minimum Bid**: Set at the Funding Goal amount (e.g., $2,000).

**Assumptions**:
- Average Funding Goal (Seed Capital): $2,500
- Average Winning Bid: $5,000 (Conservative 2x multiple due to demand)
- Auction frequency increases over time

**Year-by-Year Projections**:

| Year | Auctions/Year | Avg Winning Bid | Total Auction Revenue | Growth |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------------------|--------|
| 1 | 6 | $4,000 | $24,000 | - |
| 2 | 12 | $5,000 | $60,000 | 150% |
| 3 | 24 | $6,000 | $144,000 | 140% |
| 4 | 36 | $7,500 | $270,000 | 87% |
| 5 | 48 | $10,000 | $480,000 | 77% |

*Note: As the brand grows, the "premium" paid over the seed capital amount will likely increase significantly.*
