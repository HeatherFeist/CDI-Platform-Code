## Renovation Training Integration

### The "Build Your Space" Program

**Phase 1: Online Success (Months 1-12)**
- Prove concept online first
- Build customer base
- Generate revenue ($10K+ proven demand)
- Save for construction costs

**Phase 2: Planning (Months 12-18)**
- Feasibility study (do you have space?)
- Zoning check (is retail allowed?)
- Design consultation (10x10 vs 10x20?)
- Budget creation ($5K-50K depending on scope)
- Funding strategy (savings, loans, grants)

**Phase 3: Skills Training (Months 18-20)**
**12-Week Construction Boot Camp:**

**Weeks 1-4: Foundations**
- Safety first (OSHA 10 certification)
- Tool usage & maintenance
- Reading blueprints
- Basic carpentry (framing, walls)
- Measuring & cutting accurately

**Weeks 5-8: Systems**
- Electrical basics (wiring, outlets, lighting)
- Plumbing fundamentals (sinks, drainage)
- HVAC (heating, cooling, ventilation)
- Drywall installation & finishing
- Painting techniques

**Weeks 9-12: Finishing**
- Flooring (tile, laminate, hardwood)
- Cabinetry & shelving
- Doors & windows
- Exterior siding
- Final inspections & permits

**Instructors:**
- Licensed contractors (volunteer/paid)
- Union apprenticeship programs (partnership)
- Vocational schools (co-taught)
- YouTube supplement (video library)

**Hands-On Practice:**
- Build demo shops on nonprofit property
- Donate completed structures to low-income sellers
- Portfolio building (photos for future work)
- Certification upon completion

**Phase 4: Construction (Months 20-24)**
- Pull permits
- Build shop (DIY with contractor oversight)
- Inspections & approvals
- Interior setup (shelving, displays, POS)
- Grand opening event

**Phase 5: Hybrid Operations (Year 3+)**
- Online store continues (national reach)
- Physical shop serves local customers
- Same inventory synced across both
- Maximize revenue streams
- Hire employees (job creation!)

## Tax Benefits & Legal Structure

### Nonprofit (501c3):
**Tax Benefits:**
- No corporate income tax
- Donors get tax deductions
- Grant eligibility ($100K-$1M+/year potential)
- Property tax exemptions
- Sales tax exemptions (some states)

**Required Activities:**
- Educational programs (‚úÖ training courses)
- Charitable services (‚úÖ free to low-income)
- Community benefit (‚úÖ economic development)
- No private inurement (‚úÖ independent board)

**Annual Requirements:**
- IRS Form 990 (public disclosure)
- State charity registration
- Board meetings (quarterly minimum)
- Conflict of interest policies
- Financial transparency

### For-Profit Platform:
**Tax Treatment:**
- Normal C-corp or LLC taxation
- Can donate to nonprofit (tax deduction)
- Profit-sharing with nonprofit (partnership agreement)
- Employee stock options (incentivize team)

**Partnership Agreement:**
- Platform donates 10% of NET profits to nonprofit
- Nonprofit provides FREE access to qualifying members
- Shared branding (but separate entities)
- No self-dealing (arm's length transactions)
