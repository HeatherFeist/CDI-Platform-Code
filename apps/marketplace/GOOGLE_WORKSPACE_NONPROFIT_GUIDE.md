## üöÄ Step-by-Step Application Process

### **Step 1: Get TechSoup Validation Token**

Google requires validation through **TechSoup** (nonprofit tech donation platform).

**Go to:** https://www.techsoup.org

1. **Create TechSoup Account**
   - Organization name: Constructive Designs Inc
   - EIN: [Your nonprofit's EIN number]
   - Address: Your nonprofit's registered address
   - Contact info: Your email

2. **Upload 501(c)(3) Documentation**
   - IRS determination letter (required!)
   - Articles of incorporation
   - Proof of current operations

3. **Wait for Validation** (1-5 business days)
   - TechSoup reviews your documents
   - You'll get approved/rejected email

4. **Get Your Validation Token**
   - Once approved, request "Google Workspace for Nonprofits"
   - You'll receive a unique validation token
   - Looks like: `ABCD-1234-EFGH-5678`

### **Step 2: Apply for Google for Nonprofits**

**Go to:** https://www.google.com/nonprofits

1. **Click "Get Started"**

2. **Sign In** with your Google account
   - Use your personal Gmail or existing account
   - This will become the super admin

3. **Enter Organization Information**
   - Legal name: Constructive Designs Inc
   - Country: United States
   - EIN: [Your EIN]
   - Website: constructivedesignsinc.org

4. **Enter TechSoup Validation Token**
   - Paste the token from Step 1
   - Click "Verify"

5. **Complete Registration**
   - Review terms and conditions
   - Accept nonprofit agreement
   - Submit application

6. **Wait for Approval** (usually instant if token is valid!)

### **Step 3: Activate Google Workspace**

Once approved for Google for Nonprofits:

1. **Go to Your Dashboard**
   - https://www.google.com/nonprofits/account

2. **Click "Google Workspace"**
   - Click "Activate"

3. **Choose Your Domain**
   - Enter: constructivedesignsinc.org
   - OR purchase new domain through Google

4. **Verify Domain Ownership**
   
   **Option A - DNS Verification (Recommended):**
   ```
   1. Google gives you a TXT record
   2. Add to your domain's DNS settings
   3. Wait 24-48 hours for verification
   ```
   
   **Option B - HTML File Upload:**
   ```
   1. Download verification file
   2. Upload to your website root
   3. Verify in Google Admin Console
   ```

5. **Set Up MX Records**
   - Update your domain's MX records to point to Google
   - This routes email through Google Workspace
   - Google provides exact records to add

6. **Create User Accounts**
   ```
   admin@constructivedesignsinc.org (you)
   info@constructivedesignsinc.org
   marketplace@constructivedesignsinc.org
   support@constructivedesignsinc.org
   ```

## üö® Common Issues & Solutions

### **"I can't find my TechSoup token"**
- Login to TechSoup.org
- Go to "Account" ‚Üí "My Organization"
- Look for "Google Workspace" product request
- Token shows there after validation

### **"My nonprofit isn't validated yet"**
- Check TechSoup account for status
- Verification takes 1-5 business days
- May need additional documentation
- Contact TechSoup support if delayed

### **"Domain verification failed"**
- DNS changes take 24-48 hours to propagate
- Double-check TXT record matches exactly
- No trailing periods or extra spaces
- Try HTML file method as alternative

### **"I don't have 501(c)(3) documentation"**
- Contact IRS or your state's Secretary of State
- Request copy of determination letter
- Most states have online filing systems
- May take 2-4 weeks to receive
