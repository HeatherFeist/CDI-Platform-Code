Introduction

Welcome to Constructive Designs Marketplace ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our marketplace platform.

By using the Constructive Designs Marketplace, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

  • Email address

  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

Listing Information:

  • Product descriptions, images, and specifications
  • Pricing and inventory data
  • Shipping preferences
  • Category and condition information

Communication Data:

  • Messages between buyers and sellers
  • Customer support inquiries
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Bid information for auctions

Transaction Information:

  • Purchase history
  • Order details
  • Payment records
  • Shipping tracking information

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URLs
  • Search queries within the marketplace

Cookies and Tracking:

  • Session cookies for authentication
  • Analytics cookies to understand user behavior
  • Preference cookies to remember your settings

1.3 Information from Third Parties

Authentication Providers:

  • Google account information (if you sign in with Google)
  • Email address and profile picture from OAuth providers

Payment Processors:

  • Transaction confirmation from Stripe
  • Payment method details (last 4 digits, expiration date)

  • Provide customer support

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

  • Optimize search and recommendation algorithms
  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activity

2.3 Communication

  • Send transactional emails (order updates, messages)
  • Notify you about account activity
  • Share platform updates and new features
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Resolve disputes

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

  • Buyers see: Seller's store name, ratings, location (city/state), product listings
  • Sellers see: Buyer's name, shipping address (for order fulfillment), contact information

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services

  • Gemini AI: Product image analysis and generation (images only, no personal data)
  • Cloud Storage: Image and file hosting
  • Email Services: Transactional email delivery
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized data)

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you before your information is transferred and subject to a different privacy policy.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data
  • Secure authentication with Supabase Auth
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on database tables
  • Regular security audits and updates

Access Controls:

  • Role-based access restrictions
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Secure API endpoints with authentication

Payment Security:

  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing through Stripe
  • We do not store full credit card numbers
  • Tokenized payment methods
Important Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
  • Closed accounts: Personal data deleted within 90 days (unless legal retention required)
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Communications: Retained for 2 years for support purposes
  • Listings: Archived listings retained for 1 year
